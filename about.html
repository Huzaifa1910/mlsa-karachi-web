Establishment Story

In the spring of 2023, amidst the bustling city of Karachi, a pivotal gathering took place that would mark the birth of a transformative initiative. It was the 8th of April when five driven young men, Syed Fasih, Nadir Hussain, Muhammad Samiullah, Muhammad Habib, and Huzaifa Ghori, assembled with a purpose that resonated beyond their immediate circle of friends and colleagues.

These individuals were not merely students; they were ambassadors of their respective universities, each bearing a passion for leadership and community building. Over a shared dinner, their conversation brimmed with ideas and aspirations. The spark that ignited their collective imagination was a vision to forge a unified platform where university ambassadors across Karachi could converge, collaborate, and thrive together.

The concept they birthed that evening was the Karachi chapter of the MLSA (Metropolitan University Student Ambassadors), envisioned as the linchpin that would connect and empower ambassadors citywide. Amidst animated discussions, the crucial decision of leadership emerged as a cornerstone. With foresight and unity, they entrusted the mantle to Muhammad Samiullah of DHA Suffa University, recognizing his leadership qualities and commitment to the cause.

Supporting Samiullah were Nadir Hussain from NED University and Syed Fasih from UIT University, chosen as the technical leads for their expertise and vision. Muhammad Habib and Huzaifa Ghori took on the role of Marketing Leads, tasked with spreading the message and mission of MLSA Karachi far and wide.

On that significant day, April 8th, 2023, these five individuals laid the foundational stone of MLSA Karachi. Little did they know then that their collective determination and initiative would pave the way for a vibrant community of student ambassadors, united in purpose and driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact.
